About me

An video editor and motion designer obsessed with visual pacing.

I'm a Video Editor and Motion Designer with over 6 years of experience creating content that keeps viewers engaged from start to finish. My main focus is high retention editing, combining strong storytelling, motion graphics, UI animation, sound design, and clean pacing to make every video feel dynamic and polished.

Over the years, I've worked on a wide range of content, including Talking Head videos, documentaries, explainers, and social media content. I enjoy turning raw footage into videos that are both visually appealing and easy to follow, always adapting my editing style to match the creator's brand and audience.

I primarily work with Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro, using industry standard workflows to deliver high quality results on time. I'm always looking to improve my craft, learn new techniques, and help creators bring their ideas to life through engaging and impactful videos.
